About MHE Movers

Leading Manufacturer & Supplier of Material Handling Equipment delivering quality, strength, and innovation across India.

Who We Are

MHE Movers is a trusted name in the field of Material Handling Equipment manufacturing and supply. We specialize in Hydraulic Hand Pallet Trucks, Electric Stackers, Battery Operated Pallet Trucks, Goods Lifts, and customized industrial solutions.

With years of industry experience, we focus on delivering high-performance, durable, and cost-effective equipment that enhances warehouse productivity and operational efficiency.

Contact Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide innovative, reliable, and efficient material handling solutions that empower businesses to achieve operational excellence. We aim to deliver quality products backed by strong after-sales support and long-term partnerships.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading brand in the material handling industry across India by continuously innovating, maintaining superior quality standards, and building strong customer trust through excellence and integrity.
